About ESLint

ESLint is a large, successful open source project used by hundreds of thousands of users and downloaded millions of times each month. Being widely used, however, doesn't by itself pay the bills or provide the funds needed to invest in the project.

Combined with direct sponsorships, ESLint turned to EthicalAds to provide a little extra income. They particularly valued that EthicalAds itself is open source and the privacy aspect of not tracking users on their site and project documentation.

ESLint is a static analyzer for JavaScript code.

“Ethical Ads makes sense for open source projects like ESLint thanks to their commitment to displaying high-quality, relevant ads that pay more than other ad systems. Their commitment to privacy and transparency goes well with the spirit of open source, and we're glad to have the extra income to help pay for ongoing maintenance and development.”

Nicholas C. Zakas
ESLint creator
