How do I get started?

Simply fill out our publisher signup form with some information, and we'll get back to you right away.

How much will I make?

These numbers will vary based on your audience and topics that you write about. Generally publishers see around $2.00 per 1,000 pageviews (CPM), assuming a majority of US and European traffic.

How are you different from other ad networks?

Unlike almost every other ad network out there, we are privacy-first meaning we don't track your users. We don't set third-party cookies so you won't need a cookie banner. You can see the exact ad code that will run on your site (it's open source) and we don't allow advertisers to insert their own tracking pixels or run their code on your site.

Which advertisers will appear on my site

When you become a publisher, you'll get access to reports that show exactly which ads are appearing on your site. All our advertisers are hand-approved and aimed to developers so you won't be promoting shady products and services.

Any policies on showing ads?

When you show an ad on your site:

  • EthicalAds should be the only ad on your site.
  • The ad should appear when the user first visits, above the fold, on both desktop and mobile.
  • The ad should not disrupt the natural reading flow of the page. It should be placed above, beside or below the main content, not within it.

See our full Ad Display Policy.

How can I be paid?

We currently support payment via PayPal, OpenCollective, and Wire transfer. Our minimum payment to publishers is currently $50. We're still getting our payment systems automated, so this number will probably get lower in the future.

See our full Payout Policy.
