How do I get started?

Simply fill out our publisher signup form with some information, and we'll get back to you right away.

How much will I make?

These numbers will vary based on your audience and topics that you write about. Generally we are able to offer around $2-2.50 per 1,000 pageviews (CPM), assuming a majority of US and European traffic.

Any policies on showing ads?

When you show an ad on your site:

  • EthicalAds should be the only ad on your site
  • The ad should appear when the user first visits, above the fold, on both desktop and mobile.
  • You are welcome to display a different ad as a fallback if EthicalAds doesn't have an ad to show on your property.

How can I be paid?

We currently support payment via PayPal, OpenCollective, and Wire transfer. Our minimum payment to publishers is currently $50. We're still getting our payment systems automated, so this number will probably get lower in the future.

See the additional display and payments details in our Publisher Policy.
