Battle-tested technology

Our sponsorship platform is built on the same open source code that powers millions of views each day on our ad network. Using the same technology we can ensure your sponsor messages load reliably, with full reporting. On top of that, we provide:

  • Weighted display based on sponsorship level (larger sponsors are shown more frequently)
  • Automatically target your sponsorships to the most relevant content for your sponsors
  • Synchronize your Open Collective sponsors, so your ad rotation is always up to date with your sponsor tiers

How much does it cost?

We have pricing of $.025 CPM, or $25 per 1 million requests. There is a minimum cost of $10/mo, which you will pay if you have fewer than 400,000 requests. A sponsorship request is counted every time a sponsorship is shown.

Money-back Guarantee

If you aren't happy with our service, we will give you a full refund within the first 30 days from account sign up. We trust that we can make your sponsorship more successful.
