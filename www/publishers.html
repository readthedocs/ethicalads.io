EthicalAds Publishers

Make money on your tech site without compromising your visitors' privacy.

Simple Integration

The simplest integration is to sign up below and tell us a bit about your audience. Once approved, you put a small JavaScript snippet on your site and then start making money right away.

Relevant Ads

We don't use cookies to track users, but we match ads based on the content of your pages. This gives your users ultimate privacy and relevent ads, the best of both worlds.

Publisher Focused

We started as a publisher, and our EthicalAds still run on all Read the Docs sites. We believe in supporting open source, because we're open source devs ourselves, and our product is built with that understanding.

How much will I make?

It depends on your traffic, but we offer an industry average 70/30% split, where you keep 70% of all ad revenue. So, this generally works out to around $1.50 per 100,000 pageviews assuming a majority of US and European traffic. These numbers will vary based on your audience and topic.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our Publisher Signup form with some information, and we'll get back to you right away.
