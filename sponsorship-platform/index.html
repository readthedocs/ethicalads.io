Simple Integration

2 lines of HTML are used to integrate sponsor logos into any of your web properties. You can include metadata from the page if you want to better match your sponsors to your page content, but our defaults work pretty well also!

Increase sponsorship value

By showing each sponsor's message on the most relevant content, and showing your larger sponsors more often, you can drive increases in sponsorship revenue.

Community-focused

EthicalAds sponsorship platform respects your users privacy, and doesn't share user data with any third-party. You can work to build sustain your community without compromises.

Custom or pre-built integrations

We have a number of existing styles that you can use to style your ad to match your site. You can see a simple example that the Python Software Foundation is using to highlight their sponsors on the Python Package Index with a custom integration as well.

Battle-tested technology

Our sponsorship platform is built on the same open source code that powers millions of views each day on our ad network. Using the same technology we can ensure your sponsor messages load reliably, with full reporting. On top of that, we provide:

  • Weighted display based on sponsorship level (larger sponsors are shown more frequently)
  • Automatically target your sponsorships to the most relevant content for your sponsors
  • Synchronize your Open Collective sponsors, so your ad rotation is always up to date with your sponsor tiers

How much does it cost?

We have pricing of $.025 CPM, or $25 per 1 million requests. There is a minimum cost of $10/mo, which you will pay if you have fewer than 400,000 requests. A sponsorship request is counted every time a sponsorship is shown.

Money-back Guarantee

If you aren't happy with our service, we will give you a full refund within the first 30 days from account sign up. We trust that we can make your sponsorship more successful.
